Product-specific repair is needed because each product breaks in a different
way, even under the same engine.

  fidelity, perched-bird artwork, and scale inflation

  and sensitive-lane dialogue/compliance

  packaging truth, and masculine stealth tone

This file gives the operator:
- a dedicated image repair reminder for each product
- a dedicated video repair reminder for each product
