# BOSMAX PLATFORM POLICY RISK MATRIX v1

Authority file for practical platform and category risk routing.

Purpose:
- give BOSMAX a conservative commercial-video safety baseline
- separate low-risk from high-risk lanes
- keep platform policy handling strict without pretending false certainty

Important:
- this file is a BOSMAX operating matrix
- it is not a legal opinion
- use stricter interpretation when platform-specific certainty is incomplete

---

## 1. Risk Labels

- `SAFE` = compatible with ordinary commercial use if product truth stays accurate
- `CAUTION` = workable, but needs tight wording / disclosure / proof discipline
- `HIGH_RISK` = easy to trigger moderation, trust collapse, or category escalation
- `AVOID` = should not be default BOSMAX output

---

## 2. Platform Operating Baseline

### TikTok / TikTok Shop

Default stance:
- strong short-form commerce opportunity
- strong deception and misleading-claim risk

Safe:
- real product truth
- practical demo
- clean recommendation
- creator-native framing with visible object proof

Caution:
- synthetic human host
- beauty/wellness implication
- comparative language
- AI-modified human realism without clear viewer understanding

High Risk:
- fake testimonial
- miracle effect
- before/after implication
- fake urgency or fake interface manipulation

Avoid:
- synthetic proof of outcome
- cure/treat style claims
- fake endorsement

### Shopee / Lazada

Default stance:
- conservative marketplace truth-first mode

Safe:
- clear SKU truth
- pack shot truth
- practical use demo
- listing-consistent copy

Caution:
- AI-heavy human-led persuasion
- emotional pressure
- synthetic customer realism

High Risk:
- fake review style
- mismatched listing truth
- exaggerated efficacy

Avoid:
- unprovable health or miracle framing
- product/version mismatch

### Meta surfaces: Facebook / Instagram / Threads

Default stance:
- visual polish is tolerated
- deception and self-image risk are sensitive

Safe:
- honest product demo
- tasteful UGC or hybrid
- clean offer communication

Caution:
- beauty/wellness aspiration
- photoreal synthetic human persuasion
- emotional manipulation in personal categories

High Risk:
- negative self-image pressure
- fake lived-experience proof
- pseudo-health or pseudo-medical framing

Avoid:
- misleading transformation claims
- manipulative health shame angles

### X / Twitter

Default stance:
- direct-response can work
- deceptive commercial behavior is fragile
- healthcare and sensitive categories should be treated very conservatively

Safe:
- clean product explanation
- direct but factual CTA

Caution:
- intense sell-through tone
- synthetic spokesperson realism

High Risk:
- healthcare/wellness persuasion
- authority impersonation
- unrealistic promise framing

Avoid:
- fake expert, fake doctor, fake testimonial

## 3. Category Risk Matrix

| Category | Default BOSMAX Risk | Preferred Route |
|----------|---------------------|-----------------|
| Household | `SAFE` | `UGC` or `HYBRID` |
| Kitchen / storage / cleaning | `SAFE` | `UGC` or `HYBRID` |
| Gadget / accessory | `SAFE` to `CAUTION` | `HYBRID` |
| Fashion | `CAUTION` | `UGC` or `HYBRID` |
| Beauty | `CAUTION` to `HIGH_RISK` | `HYBRID` or `PGC` |
| Wellness | `HIGH_RISK` | `PGC` or educator-style `HYBRID` |
| Traditional / herbal | `HIGH_RISK` | `PGC` or educator-style `HYBRID` |
| Sensitive products | `HIGH_RISK` | `PGC` or educator-style `HYBRID` |

---

## 4. Synthetic Persona Risk

### Low risk use

Synthetic humans may:
- present
- guide
- demonstrate
- frame a scenario

### High risk use

Synthetic humans should not default to:
- customer testimony
- patient confession
- professional endorsement
- fake founder story

If the sale depends on "trust me, I used this", synthetic persona risk rises sharply.

---

## 5. Copy Risk

### Safe copy direction

- practical
- observable
- use-case-first
- product-truth-first
- bounded benefits

### Risky copy direction

- miracle claims
- time-bound result guarantees
- exaggerated transformation
- emotional manipulation without proof
- fake scarcity or fake review energy

---

## 6. Visual Risk

### Safer visuals

- real pack/label truth
- hand/product scale truth
- visible function proof
- creator-native but believable pacing

### Riskier visuals

- fake before/after
- impossible product effect
- synthetic over-beautification
- misleading environment or fake endorsement cues

---

## 7. BOSMAX Routing Rule

When platform or category certainty is incomplete:
- choose the stricter route
- prefer `PGC` or educator-style `HYBRID`
- lower claims
- raise proof density

If both category and platform are risky:
- do not compensate with more aggressive copy
- compensate with more restraint and more product truth

---

## 8. Operator Output Requirement

For commercial video planning, BOSMAX should be able to state:
- platform risk = `SAFE | CAUTION | HIGH_RISK | AVOID`
- category risk = `SAFE | CAUTION | HIGH_RISK | AVOID`
- route chosen = `UGC | PGC | HYBRID`
- reason for that route
